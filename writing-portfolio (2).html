I'm a community journalist telling stories that matter in Central Pennsylvania. From local businesses and cultural traditions to social impact initiatives and grassroots advocacy, I explore how everyday people are shaping their communities.

Based in Lancaster, Pennsylvania, I'm a freelance contributor to The Burg. In 2025, I received the Keystone Media Award from the Pennsylvania NewsMedia Association for Excellence in Reporting on Diversity, Equity and Inclusion.
